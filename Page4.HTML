COLOSSEUM

The COLOSSEUM is an oval amphitheatre in the centre of the city of Rome,Italy. It is situated just east of the Roman Forum built in 72 A.D.The COLOSSEUM could hold ,as estimated ,between 50,000 to 80,000 spectators. It was used for gladiatorial contests and public spectators like sea battles.The COLOSSEUM is still an iconic symbol
of imperial Rome and one of the most popular attractions of Rome.
