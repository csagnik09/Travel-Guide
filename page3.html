GREAT WALL OF CHINA

THE GREAT WALL OF CHINA is a series of fortifications made of stone,brick,tamped earth,wood and other materials.it stretches from DANDONG in the east,to the LOP LAKE in the west,along an arc an arc that roughly delineates the southern edge of INNER MONGOLIA.Especially famous is the wall built in 220-206BCE by QIN SHI HUANG,the first emperor of CHINA. It was meant to protect the chinese states and empires against the raids and invasions of the various nomadic groups of the EURASIAN STEPPE. THE GREAT WALL OF CHINA has on and off been rebuilt,maintained and enhanced;the majority of the existing wall is from the MING DYNASTY(1368-1644).


TAJ MAHAL

The TAJ MAHAL is an ivory white marble mausoleum on the southern bank of YAMUNA river in the indian city of AGRA. It was comissioned by SHAH JAHAN in 1632 by the Mughal emperor SHAH JAHAN(1628-1658) to house the tomb of his favourite wife,MUMTAZ MAHAL.TheTAJ MAHAL is the centerpiece of a complex nearly 17 hectares in area ,which includes a mosque and a guest house,and is set in the formal gardens bounded on three sides by a crenellated wall. The TAJ MAHAL was designated as a UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE in 1983.In 2007,it was declared a winner of the New Seven Wonders of the World(2000-2007)initiative.
